Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

+

To place an order, browse our collection, select the books you want to purchase, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, mobile money transfers, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely.

How long does shipping take?

+

Shipping times vary depending on your location. Local deliveries typically take 1-3 business days, while international shipping can take 7-14 business days.

Can I return a book?

+

Yes, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Books must be in their original condition. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

Do you offer e-books?

+

Yes, we offer e-books for many of our titles. Look for the e-book option on the book's detail page.

Shipping & Returns

Shipping Information

We strive to deliver your books as quickly and safely as possible. Here's what you need to know about our shipping process:

  • Orders are processed within 24-48 hours
  • Local delivery (within city) takes 1-3 business days
  • National delivery takes 3-7 business days
  • International shipping takes 7-14 business days
  • Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on location and order size
  • Free shipping on orders over $50 (local only)

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, here's our return policy:

  • Returns accepted within 14 days of delivery
  • Books must be in original condition with no damage
  • Original receipt or proof of purchase required
  • Refunds processed within 7-10 business days
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Damaged items during shipping will be replaced at no additional cost

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.
